The service

SwiPic helps you review and clean up your photo library on your device. The free tier includes 50 swipe decisions per day.

Subscription

SwiPic Pro is an auto-renewable subscription (monthly or yearly). Payment is charged to your Apple Account. The subscription renews automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage and cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings. The yearly plan may include a free trial; any unused portion of the trial is forfeited when you purchase a subscription. A one-time lifetime unlock is also available.

Your responsibility

Deleting photos is your decision. SwiPic always routes deletions through the iOS system confirmation, and deleted photos remain in Recently Deleted for 30 days — but please double-check before emptying that album.
